# Research Edge Series Articles

This directory contains all articles in the Research Edge Series, organized by publication number.

## Published Articles

### #001: Why Consumers Unconsciously Mislead Us
**File:** `001-unconscious-consumer-behavior.md`  
**Topic:** Consumer Decision-Making and Research Methodology  
**Date:** August 27, 2025

Understanding post-hoc rationalization in consumer research. Learn why consumers cannot consciously access their decision processes and how expert researchers use perception association techniques to uncover real choice drivers.

### #002: The Science of Proper Measurement
**File:** `002-measurement-fundamentals.md`  
**Topic:** Construct Measurement and Survey Design Fundamentals  
**Date:** August 27, 2025

Moving beyond casual surveys to rigorous research design. Learn the fundamental distinction between reflective and formative constructs and how measurement errors can destroy research validity.

### #003: The Sample Size Paradox
**File:** `003-sample-size-paradox.md`  
**Topic:** Statistical Sampling Theory and Precision  
**Date:** September 2025

Why statistical precision trumps intuitive mathematics. Demonstrates why population size bears minimal relationship to required sample sizes and how precision requirements should drive sampling decisions.

### #003-Supplement: Can 300 Taxi Rides Represent a Million Rides?
**File:** `003-supplement-taxi-experiment.md`  
**Topic:** Experimental Validation of Sampling Theory  
**Date:** September 20, 2025

Real-world experimental validation of the Sample Size Paradox using 1 million NYC taxi transactions.

### #004: The Trait Trap
**File:** `004-the-trait-trap.md`  
**Topic:** Causation vs. Correlation in Business Research  
**Date:** 2025

Why copying successful companies usually fails and how rigorous research fixes this. Covers reverse causality, confounding variables, and methods like regression analysis and propensity score matching.

### #005: AI and the Qualitative Analysis Problem
**File:** `005-ai-qualitative-analysis.md`  
**Topic:** AI-Assisted Qualitative Research Methodology  
**Date:** 2025

Why most AI tools flatten qualitative data. Covers rigorous qualitative analysis methods — coding, theme construction, pattern detection — and how to use AI as a structured analytical layer with full traceability.

## Article Naming Convention

Articles are named using the format: `[number]-[topic-slug].md`

- **Number:** Three-digit series number (001, 002, etc.)
- **Topic Slug:** Descriptive identifier using hyphens
